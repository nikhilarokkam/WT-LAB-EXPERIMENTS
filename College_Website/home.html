ANITS

Anil Neerukonda Institute of Technology & Sciences (ANITS),
Visakhapatnam is owned by Megha Engineering and Infrastructure Limited
(MEIL) which is one of the top infrastructure and manufacturing company
in India with headquarters at Hyderabad. The institute was established in
the academic year 2001-02 by Anil Neerukonda Educational Society
(ANES) which was founded by Dr. N.B.R. Prasad, an NRI Philanthropist
from the USA, with industrialists and eminent educationists in memory of
Late Anil Neerukonda, S/o. Dr. N.B.R. Prasad.
